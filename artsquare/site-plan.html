The website will be place to showcase poetry, painting, performing arts and gallery exhibition events calendar and open calls for publishing. The website will act as an informational bridge for both artist and the audiences. Events and Deadlines

This page will be dedicated in providing information that is up to date with regards of events that will be happening within the arts industry. The main focus of the page is to ensure that people who enjoy arts will be well informed of events that are happening within their area so they can properly plan. It will also be a place to gather artist and audience so that awareness of recently released projects could be accessed. With regards with deadlines most call to publish, audition or even perform usually are hard to verify and difficult to know. As result artist find hard to find information to help them to able to participate. This page will act as the calendar of all arts programmes happening around Zimbabwe.
